my inspiration is my mother

my mother is my inspiration She has always been a moral support for me. Her precious thoughts restrict me from doing any misdeeds. She is a support to my family. When I was a child she often used to tell me that I resemble her in many ways than my father. I never understood her words at that age. But now, when I am fully grown up I have understood her words. I truly look like her. She has given me four important lessons to follow in my life. To me these lessons are the complete package of inspiration.>
